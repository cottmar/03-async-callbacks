Washington (/ˈwɒʃɪŋtən/ (About this sound listen)), officially the State of Washington, is a state in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States.
 Named after George Washington, the first president of the United States, the state was made out of the western part of the Washington Territory, which was ceded by Britain in 1846 in accordance with the Oregon Treaty in the settlement of the Oregon boundary dispute. I
